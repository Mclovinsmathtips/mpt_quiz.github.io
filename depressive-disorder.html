Depressive Disorder Quiz

1. Do you feel sad, empty, or hopeless most of the day, nearly every day?

2. Have you lost interest or pleasure in most activities?

3. Do you have significant weight loss or gain, or a decrease or increase in appetite?

4. Do you experience insomnia or hypersomnia nearly every day?

5. Do you feel physically restless or slowed down?

6. Do you feel fatigue or loss of energy nearly every day?

7. Do you feel worthless or excessively guilty?

8. Do you have difficulty concentrating or making decisions?

9. Do you have thoughts of death or suicide?
