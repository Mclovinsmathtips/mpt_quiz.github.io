Depressive Disorder Quiz

1. Have you experienced depressed mood most of the day, nearly every day, for at least two weeks?

2. Have you lost interest or pleasure in most activities you used to enjoy?

3. Have you experienced significant weight loss or gain, or changes in appetite?

4. Have you had trouble sleeping or slept too much?

5. Have you felt fatigued or lacked energy almost every day?

6. Have you experienced feelings of worthlessness or excessive guilt?

7. Have you had difficulty concentrating or making decisions?

8. Have you had thoughts of death or suicidal ideation?
