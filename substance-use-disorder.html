Substance Use Disorder Quiz

1. Do you often take more of the substance than you intended?

2. Do you often try to cut down or stop using the substance but can't?

3. Do you spend a lot of time getting, using, or recovering from use of the substance?

4. Do you have cravings and urges to use the substance?

5. Do you fail to fulfill major roles at work, school, or home because of substance use?

6. Do you continue to use, even when it causes problems in relationships?

7. Do you give up important social, occupational, or recreational activities because of substance use?

8. Do you use substances again and again, even when it puts you in danger?

9. Do you continue to use, even when you know you have a physical or psychological problem that could have been caused or made worse by the substance?

10. Do you need more of the substance to get the effect you want (tolerance)?

11. Do you have withdrawal symptoms when the substance wears off?
